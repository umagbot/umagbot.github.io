For Students and Recent Grads

Students and recent grads in any discipline, high school or university...

JOIN A DIVISION AND/OR SUBSYSTEM

You will have the opportunity to apply knowlege learned in the classroom, gain new skills and meet new people. Reach out to us at any time of the year and we will set you up on any division or subsystem that interests you. You don't have to attend a recruitment event to join.

APPLY FOR A SPECIALIZED ROLE

Specialized roles such as on the Executive Team are typically available at the end of the school year. However, we actively seek new applications. Interviews will be conducted when positions are available to ensure the readiness of candidates for specialized roles.

For Professionals and External Organizations

Engineers, Scientists, Artists, Researchers, Policymakers, Executives, Entrepreneurs, Companies, Universities, Not-for-Profits, Professional Societies, Global Alliances, Research Agencies, Governments, etc...

JOIN AN ADVISORY BOARD

Are you passionate about our mission and think you can contribute? Join our Faculty Advisory Board or become a consultant. Your expertise would be greatly appreciated.

SPONSOR US

We are actively seeking monetary and in-kind contributions from organizations of all types across the world, whether private or public company, university, or government agency. We will ensure you get the recognition you deserve!

COLLABORATE WITH US

If you'd like to work together on an R&D project, a capstone design project/thesis (we act as industry representatives), or execute a joint initiative such as an event, reach out to us! Like with our sponsors, we ensure collaborating groups are properly recognized.
