University of Manitoba Agricultural Robotics Team

Pushing the limits of Agricultural Robotics in Manitoba

ENGINEERING FOR THE FUTURE FARMER

We are an award-winning, interdisciplinary student team committed to the development of innovative, autonomous agricultural machines and infrastructure that meet the needs of the future farmer. By participating in the agBOT Challenge, we build solutions to precision agricultural engineering problems by applying cutting-edge techniques in artificial intelligence and data analysis.

Divisions


Weed and Feed

GreenVision

Our members' motivations range from fighting climate change through advanced input management, reducing drudgery of repetitive or dangerous agricultural tasks, adding value to farming practices, or advancing their skills in embedded systems and machine learning.
